Help/FAQ

1. What is Dropshipping?

A- Dropshipping is a business model that you can use to run your store without ever holding any inventory. Once you've made a sale, your supplier will ship your products from their warehouse, straight to your customer's doorstep. You'll never need to worry about storing, packaging, or sending your products.

3. Do I need to buy the products before I sell them?

A- NO. That is the best thing about dropshipping. You only pay for the product once you have sold it and collected a payment for it. This means that you can start selling thousands of products online without even having to buy them in bulk. You do not have to worry about warehousing the products, packaging & shipping, inventory management. All this is taken care for you so you can focus on the selling the products and making and collecting payments from your buyers.

4. Do I need an Amazon Seller account to sell on Amazon?

A- YES you do need to have a Professional Amazon Seller account to sell on Amazon.

5. Do I need a Flipkart Seller account to sell on Flipkart?

A- YES you do need to have a Professional Flipkart Seller account to sell on Flipkart.

6. How do I know how much to charge for shipping?

A- We will provide you with an estimated shipping cost up front so you will know how much to charge your buyer. This cost cannot be 100% accurate since many suppliers calculate shipping based on the weight and the final shipping destination however we very rarely have to request additional funds for shipping. This is extremely rare.

7. Is there a minimum order?

A- There is no minimum order with your membership. You can order as little as one product at a time (excluding the Bulk Buys category).

8. Do you provide an inventory feed?

A- Free inventory files are included in your membership. Inventory files are available in CSV and XML formats and are updated several times daily. You can get our full inventory file or create your own custom file. We also have pre-formatted templates for Shopify, Bigcommerce, Amazon, Flipkart and more...

9. Will I get a tracking number when my order has been shipped?

A- Yes. Shipping and tracking information will be available after your order has been shipped.

10. How long does it take to receive an order?

Orders process and ship within 1-2 business days. Orders typically arrive within 5-10 days.

In general, if an item is broken or defective, we will send out a replacement or issue credit to your account. Non-defective/non-damaged returns must be shipped back to us within 30 days and we will credit your account minus a 20% restocking fee.
